Netty 4.0.7.Final released

by normanmaurer
on 08-Aug-2013

Only one week after the last release we though it is about time to bring you another one. This is a bug-fix release but also includes performance improvements.

In summary we closed 10 issues.

This release would have been not possible without the feedback of our community. We welcome everyone to become a part of it, and help us to make Netty even better!

Important changes

  • Channel.isWritable() does not work as aspected when write from outside the EventLoop (#1697) and (#1702)
  • SwappedByteBuf.readSlice(int) not respect ByteOrder. (#1704)
  • Allow to also write FileRegion and ChunkInput when using HTTP chunked. (#1693) and (#1699)

Visit here for the complete list of the changes.

As always please let us know if you find any issues.

Every idea and bug-report counts and so we thought it is worth mentioning those who helped in this area. Please report an unintended omission.
